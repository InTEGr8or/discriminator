The Discriminator
Def: moral differentiation

Everyone discriminates. If you're shocked by this statement, and say "I would never discriminate!", that is because you discriminate against discrimination.

Discrimination just means to judge one thing to be good and another to be bad.

Although this list may appear to reflect political groups, that similarity is only circumstantial, local, and temporary. It is important to note, however, that politics must rightfully be based on morality/ethics.
